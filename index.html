About

"Respectful Respawn" tackles the problem of toxicity in gaming, where hurtful behavior makes the experience unpleasant for many players. This negativity can lead to bullying and stress, causing people to feel unwelcome. We promote respect and support to help everyone feel safe and valued in the gaming community. Together, we can make gaming a positive place for all!

Toxic Behaviors

This is a problems since about 25% of players have experience toxic behavior if many different ways.

League of Legend

In League of Legends, toxic players can make the game less enjoyable, causing stress and burnout for many. This behavior can also lead to unfair play, hurting the overall experience for everyone.

Overwatch

Toxic interactions in Overwatch can break down teamwork, which is key to winning. This can also make the game less friendly for diverse players, limiting who feels welcome in the community.
